About VPS

A safe, supportive space for men experiencing domestic and marital abuse.

Our mission

VPS exists to ensure that every man facing domestic or marital abuse is heard, believed, and supported. We reduce stigma, provide resources, and build community so no one has to walk alone.

Our services include anonymous story sharing, peer connections, and access to trained counselors.
